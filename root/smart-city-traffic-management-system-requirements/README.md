# Smart City Traffic Management System Requirements

## Group Overview
This document group contains the requirements specification for the Smart City Traffic Management System (SCTMS), an AI-driven platform designed to optimize urban traffic flow, reduce congestion, and enhance road safety. The group is managed by the Smart Mobility Solutions team and supports the development and deployment of a next-generation traffic control system.

## Core Domain & Boundaries
**Belongs in this group:**
- Requirements specifications, use cases, and functional/non-functional requirements for the Smart City Traffic Management System.
- Technical design documents related to AI, IoT, computer vision, and traffic signal control.
- Compliance and security documentation covering GDPR, CCPA, TLS, and cybersecurity standards.
- Integration specifications for NTCIP-compliant controllers, RESTful APIs, and cloud/edge computing environments.

**Does NOT belong in this group:**
- Documents related to unrelated domains such as public transportation scheduling, city-wide energy management, or urban planning.
- Documents focused on hardware procurement, infrastructure deployment, or third-party vendor contracts unless directly tied to system requirements.
- Documents from other projects or initiatives not related to the Smart City Traffic Management System.
